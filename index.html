    Basic English Grammar - Level 1

    ENGLISH EASY 3 Questions

    Test your understanding of basic English verbs, nouns, and sentence structures. Perfect for beginners looking to improve their language skills.

    English Test, Grammar Check, Beginner English, Language Assessment, ESL Quiz, Basic Verbs, Simple Nouns, English Level 1, School English
    Which word is a verb in this sentence: 'She runs fast'?
    Runs
    She
    Fast
    Explanation: 'Runs' is the action word in the sentence, making it the verb.
    Choose the correct plural form of 'Child'.
    Childs
    Children
    Childrens
    Explanation: 'Children' is the irregular plural form of 'Child'.
    What is the opposite of 'Happy'?
    Joyful
    Glad
    Sad
    Explanation: 'Sad' is the antonym (opposite) of 'Happy'.

    Parts of Speech - English Quiz

    GRAMMAR MEDIUM 3 Questions

    Challenge yourself with this quiz on Parts of Speech. Identify nouns, adjectives, and adverbs correctly to score high points.

    Parts of Speech, Adjectives and Adverbs, Identify Nouns, English Grammar Test, Intermediate English, Grammar Exercise, Syntax Quiz, Language Mechanics
    In the phrase 'Red Apple', what part of speech is 'Red'?
    Noun
    Adjective
    Verb
    Explanation: 'Red' describes the apple, so it is an Adjective.
    Select the Proper Noun:
    London
    City
    Town
    Explanation: 'London' is the specific name of a place, making it a Proper Noun.
    Identify the Adverb: 'He spoke softly.'
    He
    Spoke
    Softly
    Explanation: 'Softly' describes how he spoke, making it an Adverb.
